Birthday Prayer from Sir.BM

May The Lord be your shepherd; therefore you shall not want.
May He make you lie down in green pastures; may He lead you beside the still waters.
May He restore your soul whenever you are weary; may He strengthen you.
May He lead you in the paths of righteousness for His name's sake.
Though you walk through the valley of the shadow of death,
you will fear no evil; for the Lord is your Shield and Buckler.
May His rod and staff comfort you.
May the Lord prepare a table before you in the presence of your enemies.
May your cup run over.
Surely goodness and mercy shall follow you all the days of your life,
and you will dwell in the house of the Lord forever.
With long life, He will satisfy you.
